# Play Store Listing

Health-content posture, deliberately: JudgeMyCal is a **wellness companion**,
never clinical, never diagnostic, never shaming. The personas are affectionate
character comedy, and every piece of copy below is written so an automated (or
human) policy reviewer reads "supportive companion", not "calorie obsession".
The safety floor is a selling point — say so.

## App title (30 chars max)

> JudgeMyCal: Meal & Move Buddy

## Short description (80 chars max)

> A kind health companion: snap meals, get honest estimates, move your way.

## Full description (4000 chars max)

> **Meet the health companion that judges your meals — never you.**
>
> JudgeMyCal is a wellness buddy with personality. Snap a photo of your plate
> and your chosen companion — supportive **Buddy**, loving **Auntie**, or
> no-nonsense **Coach** — reacts with warmth, humour, and an honest calorie
> estimate.
>
> **Honest numbers, always.** Photo calorie estimates are genuinely uncertain,
> so JudgeMyCal never pretends otherwise: every estimate is a range, with a
> clear confidence level and the item we're least sure about. One tap corrects
> a portion and tightens the range.
>
> **Three companions, one memory.** Buddy is the default — unconditionally
> supportive. Auntie guilt-trips you with love. Coach keeps it direct. Switch
> any time; your log and history never change, only the voice does.
>
> **A safety floor that can't be switched off.** The personas tease your food
> choices, never you. And if you're having a hard moment, every persona stops
> being a character and points you to real support. This is built into the
> app's foundations, not a setting.
>
> **Beyond the plate:**
> • Shopping swaps — add your basket items and get neutral, goal-friendly
>   alternatives. No food is ever called "bad". Suggestions only; JudgeMyCal
>   never buys anything.
> • Workouts that respect rest — tell it your goal, time, and equipment, and
>   get a warm-up/main/cool-down session. Rest days are treated as a healthy
>   choice, never a broken streak.
>
> **Private by design.** Anonymous sign-in — no account, no email, no ads.
> Meal photos are processed to estimate your meal and nothing else. Works in
> offline demo mode too.
>
> JudgeMyCal is a wellness companion, not a medical or diagnostic tool.
